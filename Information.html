Welcome to

Sport Explorer

On this website you will be able to find various sports, from which you can choose whatever one you like.

Me and my team made sure to add as many different sports as possible, in order to satisfy as many customers as possible.

In the header you will find our logo, 2 buttons and a burger menu. When you click on the logo, you will be redirected to

the home page. The minigame button is not functional for now, but I can assure you the whole team is working really hard on it

The info button will redirect you to this page right here when you are in another one. The burger menu will drop a list with all

the sports when clicked and you can choose from there, if it is more convenient for you. In order to actually start browsing the

sports, you have to go to the home page and click on the "Lets Explore" button. After that you will be greeted by a world map with

a lot of markers, representing each sport and it's origin. When you click on the marker, you'll be redirected to a page with brief

explanation about the sport, the rules, etc. and a visual representation of the sport.
